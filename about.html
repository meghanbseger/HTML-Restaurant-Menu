Origin Story

Welcome! We are a proud 3 generational family owned buisness. We moved from Ko Semet, Thailand which is located on Thailand's East Coast. We won Ko Semet's Favorite Resaurant in 2010, 2011, 2012, 2013, 2014 and 2015. 5 years in row we were our Risui's favorite restaurant! We had many customers including tourists and our cities residents. In 2015 we decided that we wanted to move our business to the United States. We are happy to serve our favorite traditional dishes with people all over the United States and we hope you enjoy our service. Thank you so much for dining with us!
